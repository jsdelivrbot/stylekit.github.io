## Graphics:

<img width="298" alt="img" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/stylekit/img/master/graphics_parts.png">

**A Graphic has 2 parts:**
- Fill
- Stroke 

By dividing the graphics into two parts we can independently update the design.
